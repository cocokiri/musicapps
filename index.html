RealTime Pitch Detection and Visualization

P l a y

JS

I made this intuitive, realtime spectrogram because all other pitch detection and spectrogram products on web/mobile were horrible.

Eg bad mappings (hello audacity), dumb Signal Processing code (why dafuq would I need autocorrelation for monophonics, Boris Smus?) and were generally cluttered (popular YIN algorithm -- 1200ms delay @ 70% accuracy. 1000 lines of C# for jack shit. No thanks).

You know it's bad when you see [frequency <-> note] mappings instead of translator functions (just logarithms, man).

The core pitch detection algorithm is extremly accurate for single harmonic sounds and has almost no delay. I have to refactor the code tho...it's a mess.

+ Shows pitch and timbre exactly where it occurs in the spectrogram
+ Gives you a strong sense of intervals, fluctations and musical relationships
+ Even if tonedeaf, you see what's up and train your voice and ear.

It will ask you for microphone access. Might not work on all browsers.
